About Me

I am a product and instructional designer in Texas. I’m currently working full-time as an instructional designer for Texas State Technical College, a technical and innovative community college.

My focus as a designer is to understand my users, their needs, and create solutions and positive experiences that connect to their goals. I’m passionate about technology, books, and living life to its fullest!.
